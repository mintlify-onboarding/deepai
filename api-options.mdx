---
title: "API Options"
---

<ParamField body="grid_size">
  Pass a string, either "1" or "2" <br />
  Pass “1” to only receive 1 image in response. <br />
  With the default, 4 will be returned
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="width, height" >
Pass a string, eg "256" or "768" (default 512) <br />
Use values between 128 and 1536. <br />

<Note>
  Values above approximately 700 or below 256 may produce strange outputs.
</Note>

</ParamField>

<ParamField body="negative_prompt">
  Pass a string to indicate what you want to be removed from the image <br />
  Can be used to enhance image quality and details.

Example negative prompts: bad anatomy,bad proportions, blurry, cloned face,
cropped, deformed, dehydrated, disfigured, duplicate, error, extra arms, extra
fingers, extra legs, extra limbs, fused fingers, gross proportions, jpeg
artifacts, long neck, low quality, lowres, malformed limbs, missing arms,
missing legs, morbid, mutated hands, mutation, mutilated, out of frame, poorly
drawn face, poorly drawn hands, signature, text, too many fingers, ugly,
username, watermark, worst quality.

</ParamField>
